BULLYING

Bullying can happen anywhere, even online.

What is Bullying?

Bullying is when someone is picked on or hurt over and over. It can be physical, verbal, or online. It harms mental health and can happen to anyone. We need to recognize it and stop it.

Bullying isn't just words; it can be physical too.

STOP BULLYING NOW

How Bullying Affects Us

Bullying makes people feel alone, anxious, and sad. It lowers self-esteem and can mess with school and friendships. We need to support each other and take action to stop it.

Respect others, online and offline.

What You Can Do

If you see bullying, speak up! Support the person being bullied, tell an adult, or just show them they're not alone. Every action counts in stopping bullying.
